About WAADA

Our Vision

The Washington Arts and Design Association (WAADA) is a nonprofit organization based in Maryland. We are committed to cultivating appreciation for the arts within Chinese-American communities and fostering creative expression across generations.

Through education, collaboration, and public engagement, WAADA connects artists, designers, and audiences to promote cross-cultural understanding and artistic excellence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower creativity and preserve cultural heritage by supporting artists through exhibitions, educational programs, and community collaborations. We aim to provide an inclusive platform where art becomes a bridge that connects people, inspires dialogue, and strengthens cultural identity.

WAADA strives to make art accessible to everyone — from emerging talents to seasoned professionals — fostering growth, appreciation, and meaningful engagement across communities.
