About WAADA

The Washington Art and Design Association (WAADA) is a nonprofit organization originating in Maryland, USA. The platform focuses on advocating the development of contemporary culture, art, and design.

Through art and design exhibitions and experiential projects, we promote cultural exchanges and cooperation between different ethnic communities and the mainstream society, attract outstanding artists, designers, and other cultural and education professionals from around the world, and serve American society and individuals to promote mutual understanding and interaction.

WAADA adheres to the values of universal civilization, upholds free thought and new concepts, and spreads creative masterpieces and advanced educational ideas.
