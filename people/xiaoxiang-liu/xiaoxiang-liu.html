About Xiaoxiang Liu

Xiaoxiang Liu is a board member at WAADA, a graphic designer based in New Jersey, USA, Art Director of XXL Studio, and a member of the Alliance Graphique Internationale (AGI). He focuses on book design and curates design exhibitions for WAADA, including international showcases such as the Sweden–China Book Design Exhibition and other major professional design events.

Selected Works

  • From a Character to a Book: Chinese Typography, Higher Education Press, 2017.
  • 11×16 XXL Studio, Shanghai People’s Fine Arts Publishing House, 2018.
  • Chinese Typography Grid Systems & Composition, Shanghai People’s Fine Arts Publishing House, 2023.

  • ADC 97th Bronze Cubes Awards.
