About George King

Born and raised in Hong Kong, George King obtained a Bachelor of Arts in Geography and a Postgraduate Diploma in Education from the University of Hong Kong. He later earned a Master of Science in Landscape Ecology, Design and Maintenance from the University of London.

George began his career as a Landscape Officer, monitoring new towns’ landscaping projects. He later served as a high school teacher for nearly 30 years, contributing extensively to education.

In social and public service, George worked as a Member of the Panel of Film Censorship Advisers at the Office for Film, Newspaper and Article Administration, Hong Kong Government (2010 – 2013). He also served as Curriculum Consultant for several web-based teacher-development courses for the Education Bureau of the Hong Kong Government (2001 – 2009), and as a Member of the School Management Board (2014 – 2019).
