Blossom in June

COME, JOIN US FOR SKETCHING AND PAINTING!

Join us for a delightful sketching and painting experience organized by the Washington Arts and Design Association. This annual community cultural and art promotion activity is led by professional art teachers and provides a wonderful opportunity for participants of all ages and skill levels to learn about painting.

Art supplies are provided, and participants will have the chance to connect with nature, express their love for the environment, and enhance their cognitive and expressive painting abilities. The event includes sharing sessions, lectures, and interactive activities to improve aesthetic appreciation.
